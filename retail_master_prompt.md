# Retail Website Copy – Master Prompt Framework

## Purpose

This prompt system is designed to generate high-converting website copy for local retail businesses.

It follows direct response copywriting principles and produces structured, ready-to-publish website content.

The system is reusable and adaptable for different business types, tones, and audiences.

---

## Master Prompt Template


Act as a specialized Direct Response Copywriter for local retail businesses.

Generate high-converting website copy for:

Business Name: [INSERT NAME]
Location: [INSERT LOCATION]
Target Audience: [INSERT AUDIENCE]
Brand Voice: [ENERGETIC / PROFESSIONAL / FRIENDLY / STUDENT-FIRST]
Unique Selling Point: [WHAT MAKES THIS BUSINESS DIFFERENT]

Constraints:

Use short sentences.

Keep language clear and relatable.

Avoid generic AI phrases.

Avoid overly formal wording.

Focus on real customer benefits.

Highlight convenience and accessibility.

Make content ready for real website publishing.

Deliver the following sections:

Hero Section

A strong headline (must mention the location clearly)

A persuasive subheadline

A short supporting paragraph (max 80–100 words)

Product or Service Categories
Create 3 category sections.
Each section must include:

Category title

2–3 short persuasive lines explaining benefits

Customer Guarantee Section

3 trust-building bullet points

Focus on reliability, value, and customer-first service

Call-to-Action Section

2 CTA button options

1 urgency-based CTA line

Clear next step for customers

Tone must match the selected Brand Voice.
Output should be structured and clean.
Do not include explanations. Only provide website-ready copy.


---

## Why This Prompt Works

This framework:

- Uses structured variables for adaptability
- Forces clear deliverables
- Controls tone and length
- Prevents generic AI output
- Ensures persuasive, conversion-focused content
- Can be reused for multiple local retail businesses
